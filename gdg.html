Google Developer Student Club, NMIT!

We're a supportive community of students passionate about learning, building, and growing together through Google's tools and technologies. Whether you're just starting out or looking to expand your skillset, you'll find a space here to develop your knowledge and apply it practically alongside like-minded peers.

In our community, you'll find webinars, hands-on workshops, tutorials, and informative talks designed to help you dive deep into various topics, collaborate on projects, and network with fellow developers.

Ready to Join?

  • Hit the “LOGIN” button above.
  • Log in and complete your profile if prompted.
  • That's it! Welcome aboard!

Once you're in, keep an eye on your inbox. We send regular updates about upcoming events, workshops, and opportunities to get involved.

Got questions or ideas? Don't hesitate to reach out! You can find our contact infomation at the bottom of this page. Let's connect and build something incredible together!
